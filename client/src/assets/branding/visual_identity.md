## 1. DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

Pressure Play looks like what happens when Bloomberg, Nike, and a Dubai penthouse have a meeting. Every visual output must feel:

- **Premium** — editorial, cinematic, intentional
- **Clean** — generous whitespace, flat compositions, no clutter
- **Dual-world** — sport and business live side by side in every frame
- **Data-informed** — numbers, stats, and overlays are part of our visual DNA
- **Dark-first** — Midnight background is our default canvas

**The Test:** If it could belong to any other podcast — it's not Pressure Play. If it feels like a magazine spread from the future — ship it.

## 2. COLOR SYSTEM

### 2.1 Primary Palette

| Swatch | Name | Hex | RGB | Usage |
|--------|------|-----|-----|-------|
| ⬛ | **Midnight** | `#0D1117` | 13, 17, 23 | Primary background, dark UI, text on light |
| 🔵 | **Electric Blue** | `#0A84FF` | 10, 132, 255 | Primary accent — links, highlights, key elements |
| 🟢 | **Signal Teal** | `#00D4AA` | 0, 212, 170 | Secondary accent — gradients, secondary highlights |
| ⬜ | **Clean White** | `#F8F9FA` | 248, 249, 250 | Body text on dark backgrounds, light backgrounds |

### 2.2 Supporting Palette

| Swatch | Name | Hex | RGB | Usage |
|--------|------|-----|-----|-------|
| 🩶 | **Slate** | `#8B949E` | 139, 148, 158 | Secondary text, captions, metadata |
| 🟡 | **Amber** | `#F0A500` | 240, 165, 0 | CTAs, alerts, urgent highlights, "new" badges |
| ⬛ | **Deep Black** | `#010409` | 1, 4, 9 | Ultra-dark backgrounds, cinematic overlays |
| ⬜ | **Soft Gray** | `#161B22` | 22, 27, 34 | Card backgrounds, panels on Midnight |

### 2.3 Brand Gradient

```
Electric Blue (#0A84FF) → Signal Teal (#00D4AA)
Direction: Left to right (default) or top-left to bottom-right (diagonal)
```

**Gradient Rules:**
- ✅ Use on: logo mark, accent bars, highlight underlines, buttons, small graphic elements
- ✅ Use on: diamond motif fills, icon accents, progress bars
- ❌ Never on: full backgrounds, large fills, body text, entire cards
- ❌ Never on: photography overlays or as a filter on images

### 2.4 Color Ratios

For any composition, maintain these approximate ratios:

| Element | Color | Ratio |
|---------|-------|-------|
| Background | Midnight / Deep Black | 60–70% |
| Content | Clean White / Slate | 20–25% |
| Accent | Electric Blue / Signal Teal / Gradient | 5–10% |
| Highlight | Amber | 1–3% (only when needed) |

### 2.5 Accessibility

- Body text on Midnight: always Clean White (`#F8F9FA`) — contrast ratio 15.4:1
- Secondary text on Midnight: Slate (`#8B949E`) — contrast ratio 5.2:1 (AA compliant)
- Never place Electric Blue text on Midnight without a weight of 500+ (readability)
- Amber on Midnight: use sparingly and at larger sizes only

## 3. TYPOGRAPHY

### 3.1 Typeface System

| Typeface | Role | Where to Get It |
|----------|------|-----------------|
| **Inter** | Primary typeface — all headings and body text | Google Fonts (free) |
| **JetBrains Mono** | Data typeface — numbers, stats, timestamps, codes | JetBrains (free) |

**Rule:** These are the only two typefaces. No exceptions. No substitutions.

### 3.2 Heading Styles

| Level | Typeface | Weight | Size (Desktop) | Case | Letter Spacing |
|-------|----------|--------|-----------------|------|----------------|
| H1 — Title | Inter | 600 (Semi-Bold) | 48–64px | Title Case | +1.5% |
| H2 — Section | Inter | 500 (Medium) | 32–40px | Title Case | +1% |
| H3 — Subsection | Inter | 500 (Medium) | 24–28px | Title Case | +0.5% |
| H4 — Label | Inter | 500 (Medium) | 18–20px | UPPERCASE | +3% |

### 3.3 Body Styles

| Style | Typeface | Weight | Size | Line Height |
|-------|----------|--------|------|-------------|
| Body Large | Inter | 400 (Regular) | 18px | 1.75 |
| Body Default | Inter | 400 (Regular) | 16px | 1.7 |
| Body Small | Inter | 400 (Regular) | 14px | 1.6 |
| Caption | Inter | 400 (Regular) | 12px | 1.5 |

### 3.4 Data Styles

| Style | Typeface | Weight | Size | Usage |
|-------|----------|--------|------|-------|
| Stat Large | JetBrains Mono | 700 | 48–72px | Hero statistics, big numbers |
| Stat Medium | JetBrains Mono | 500 | 24–32px | Inline stats, data callouts |
| Stat Small | JetBrains Mono | 400 | 14–16px | Timestamps, episode numbers, metadata |

### 3.5 Typography Rules

- Headings: never go below 500 weight
- Body: never go above 400 weight (keep it light and readable)
- Numbers in any context: always JetBrains Mono (episode numbers, dates, stats, scores, percentages)
- Maximum 2 font sizes per composition (excluding data overlays)
- Line length: 60–80 characters for body text (optimal readability)
- Paragraph spacing: 1.5x the body font size

## 4. LOGO SYSTEM

### 4.1 Logo Variants

| Variant | Description | Minimum Width | Use Case |
|---------|-------------|---------------|----------|
| **Full Lockup** | Diamond mark + "PRESSURE PLAY" wordmark (horizontal) | 200px | Website headers, video intros, press kits, sponsor decks |
| **Stacked Lockup** | Diamond mark above "PRESSURE PLAY" wordmark | 120px | Podcast cover art, vertical layouts, merch |
| **Icon Mark** | Diamond with embedded play triangle | 32px | Favicons, app icons, social avatars, watermarks |
| **Monogram** | PP letters | 24px | Merch embroidery, thumbnail corners, subtle branding |

### 4.2 Logo Construction

The diamond mark combines two symbols:
- **Diamond shape** — represents "pressure creates diamonds"
- **Play triangle** — represents media, motion, content

The mark uses the brand gradient (Electric Blue → Signal Teal).
The wordmark uses Inter Medium, tracked at +3%, in Clean White (dark backgrounds) or Midnight (light backgrounds).

### 4.3 Clear Space

Minimum clear space around any logo variant = **1x the height of the diamond mark** on all sides. No other element may enter this zone.

```
Example (icon mark is 40px tall):

        40px clear
    ┌─────────────────┐
    │                 │
40px│    💎 ICON      │ 40px
    │                 │
    └─────────────────┘
        40px clear
```

### 4.4 Logo Placement Rules

| Background | Logo Version |
|------------|--------------|
| Midnight / dark image | Full-color gradient mark + Clean White wordmark |
| Clean White / light | Full-color gradient mark + Midnight wordmark |
| Photography | Place on darkest area OR use a subtle dark overlay behind logo |
| Video | Bottom-right corner, 70% opacity, icon mark or monogram only |

### 4.5 Logo Don'ts

- ❌ Never stretch, squish, or rotate the logo
- ❌ Never change the gradient direction or colors
- ❌ Never place on busy backgrounds without an overlay
- ❌ Never add drop shadows, outlines, or effects
- ❌ Never recreate the logo in a different typeface
- ❌ Never use the wordmark without the mark (or vice versa) in formal contexts
- ❌ Never scale below minimum width

## 5. IMAGERY & PHOTOGRAPHY

### 5.1 Photography Style

| Attribute | Direction |
|-----------|-----------|
| **Mood** | Cinematic, high-contrast, editorial |
| **Lighting** | Dramatic — strong directional light, deep shadows |
| **Color grading** | Cool tones (blue/teal shadows), desaturated midtones, clean highlights |
| **Focus** | Sharp subject, subtle depth of field on background |
| **Composition** | Rule of thirds, generous negative space, room for text overlays |

### 5.2 Subject Matter

**Use:**
- Athletes in motion or intense focus (never posed/cheesy)
- Business environments: boardrooms, skylines, trading floors, screens with data
- Dubai cityscape (Burj Khalifa, DIFC, Marina — as recurring visual backdrop)
- Close-up details: hands gripping, eyes focused, sweat, watches, screens
- Technology: clean desk setups, code on screens, AI interfaces
- Split-screen compositions: sport on one side, business on the other

**Never Use:**
- Generic stock photography (handshakes, thumbs up, smiling-at-laptop)
- Over-filtered or heavily processed images
- Low-resolution or pixelated photos
- Posed, stiff, or unnatural shots
- Cartoons, illustrations, or clip art (unless custom-commissioned)

### 5.3 Image Treatment

When placing images in layouts:

- **Default:** Full-bleed on Midnight background with subtle vignette
- **Overlay:** 40–60% Midnight overlay on images when placing text on top
- **Duotone:** Electric Blue + Midnight duotone for stylized feature images (use sparingly)
- **Crop:** Always crop with intention — tight crops on faces for emotion, wide crops for environment
- **Border:** Never use borders on images. Let them bleed or float.

## 6. GRAPHIC ELEMENTS

### 6.1 Diamond Motif

The diamond is our signature graphic element. Use it as:

- A background pattern (outlined diamonds, 5–10% opacity, repeated grid)
- A frame for key statistics or quotes
- A section divider (small diamond icon centered)
- A highlight marker (small filled diamond before list items)

**Rules:**
- Always use brand gradient fill OR Clean White outline
- Never fill with solid colors outside the palette
- Scale proportionally — never stretch into rectangles

### 6.2 Grid & Lines

- Use subtle grid patterns (1px lines, 3–5% opacity) on backgrounds for a "data/tech" feel
- Horizontal divider lines: 1px, Slate color, with generous padding above and below
- Use angular/diagonal lines sparingly for energy — always at 45° or matching the diamond angle

### 6.3 Data Overlays

Stats and numbers are part of our visual language. When overlaying data on images or video:

- Use JetBrains Mono
- Semi-transparent Midnight background pill behind the number
- Electric Blue or Signal Teal accent on the number itself
- Position in corners or along edges — never center-screen blocking the subject

```
Example:

┌──────────────────────────────────┐
│                                  │
│   [CINEMATIC IMAGE]              │
│                                  │
│                    ┌───────────┐ │
│                    │ 67% ▲     │ │
│                    │ win rate  │ │
│                    └───────────┘ │
└──────────────────────────────────┘
```

### 6.4 Quote Cards

For social media quote graphics:

- Background: Midnight (`#0D1117`)
- Quote text: Clean White, Inter Medium, 24–32px
- Attribution: Slate, Inter Regular, 14–16px
- Accent: 3px vertical bar on the left in gradient (Electric Blue → Signal Teal)
- Diamond icon or PP monogram in bottom corner
- Generous padding: minimum 60px on all sides

## 7. YOUTUBE THUMBNAILS

### 7.1 Specifications

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Dimensions** | 1280 × 720px |
| **Aspect ratio** | 16:9 |
| **Format** | JPG or PNG |
| **Max file size** | 2MB |

### 7.2 Thumbnail Formula

Every thumbnail must include exactly:

1. **A face** — host or guest, showing clear emotion (intensity, surprise, confidence, focus)
2. **Text** — 2–4 words maximum. Inter Semi-Bold. High contrast against background.
3. **Brand mark** — PP monogram or diamond icon in bottom-right corner (small, subtle, ~40px)
4. **Color accent** — at least one element in Electric Blue, Signal Teal, or gradient

### 7.3 Thumbnail Layouts

**Layout A — Solo Guest**
```
┌────────────────────────────────┐
│ MIDNIGHT BG                    │
│                                │
│   "THE REAL     [GUEST FACE   │
│    REASON"       CUTOUT,      │
│                  RIGHT SIDE]  │
│              💎 PP             │
└────────────────────────────────┘
```

**Layout B — Split Screen**
```
┌───────────────┬────────────────┐

```

**Layout C — Dual Hosts**
```
┌────────────────────────────────┐

│   LEFT     TEXT      RIGHT    │
│            CENTER             │
```

### 7.4 Thumbnail Don'ts

- ❌ Red arrows, circles, or clickbait graphics
- ❌ More than 4 words of text
- ❌ Cluttered compositions
- ❌ Low-resolution or blurry images
- ❌ Faces smaller than 30% of the frame
- ❌ Text smaller than 48px
- ❌ Bright/neon backgrounds

## 8. SOCIAL MEDIA TEMPLATES

### 8.1 Instagram / TikTok (Vertical — 1080 × 1920px)

**Clip Template:**
- On-screen text: Inter Medium, Clean White, centered or top-third
- Text background: semi-transparent Midnight pill or no background if image is dark enough
- Lower third: gradient bar (2px) + guest name in Inter Medium + topic in Slate
- PP monogram watermark: top-right corner, 60% opacity

**Carousel Template (1080 × 1080px):**
- Slide 1: Hook text on Midnight background, gradient accent
- Slides 2–9: One insight per slide, clean layout, consistent positioning
- Final slide: CTA + handles + diamond icon

### 8.2 X / Twitter

**Quote Graphic (1200 × 675px):**
- Midnight background
- Quote in Clean White, Inter Medium, 28–36px
- Left accent bar: 4px, gradient
- Attribution below in Slate
- PP monogram bottom-right

**Thread Header (1200 × 675px):**
- Midnight background
- Thread title: large Inter Semi-Bold
- "🧵 THREAD" label: JetBrains Mono, Electric Blue
- Episode number: JetBrains Mono, Slate

### 8.3 YouTube Community / LinkedIn (1200 × 675px)

- Same design language as X graphics
- Slightly more text-friendly (can include 1–2 sentence caption on the image)
- Always include the full logo lockup (not just monogram)

## 9. VIDEO & MOTION

### 9.1 Video Color Grading

| Parameter | Direction |
|-----------|-----------|
| **Shadows** | Push toward blue/teal (brand colors) |
| **Midtones** | Slightly desaturated, neutral |
| **Highlights** | Clean, not blown out |
| **Contrast** | High — cinematic feel |
| **Overall** | Cool-toned, premium, editorial |

### 9.2 Lower Thirds

```
┌────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ ▌ PATRICE EVRA                             │
│ ▌ Former Man United Captain & Entrepreneur │
└────────────────────────────────────────────┘
  ↑ gradient bar (3px)
```

- Background: Midnight at 85% opacity with subtle blur
- Name: Inter Medium, Clean White, 20px
- Title: Inter Regular, Slate, 14px
- Left accent: 3px gradient bar (Electric Blue → Signal Teal)
- Animation: slide in from left, hold 5 seconds, fade out

### 9.3 Title Cards

- Midnight background
- Episode number: JetBrains Mono, Slate, top-left
- Episode title: Inter Semi-Bold, Clean White, centered
- Gradient underline beneath title (2px)
- Diamond motif pattern at 5% opacity in background

### 9.4 Transitions

- **Default:** Clean cut (no transitions between interview segments)
- **Between segments:** 0.5s fade through Midnight
- **Highlight moments:** Quick zoom (105% over 0.3s) on impactful statements
- **Never use:** Star wipes, spins, page turns, or any "template" transitions

### 9.5 Text on Screen (During Clips)

- Typeface: Inter Medium
- Color: Clean White with subtle Midnight drop shadow (for legibility)
- Position: Center-bottom third of frame
- Size: 42–56px for emphasis, 32px for supporting text
- Animation: Pop in word-by-word or line-by-line (subtle scale from 95% → 100%)
- Duration: Minimum 2 seconds per text block on screen

## 10. PRINT & MERCH

### 10.1 Print Defaults

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Paper | Midnight-equivalent (rich black) or Clean White |
| Ink | Gradient foil for diamond mark, white ink for text on dark |
| Finish | Matte default, spot UV on logo/diamond elements |

### 10.2 Merch Guidelines

- **Colorways:** Midnight (primary), Clean White (secondary), Slate (limited)
- **Logo placement:** left chest (icon mark), center back (full lockup or PP monogram)
- **Tags:** custom woven label with diamond icon and "Pressure Play" in Inter
- **Quality:** premium blanks only — heavyweight cotton, structured caps, quality embroidery
- **No:** all-over prints, neon colors, cheap screen prints, busy designs

### 10.3 Merch Phrases (Approved for Print)

- PRESSURE PLAY (wordmark only)
- 💎 (diamond icon standalone)
- PP (monogram standalone)
- "Pressure Creates Diamonds"
- "Performance Has No Off-Season"
- "Same Pressure. Different Arena."
- Episode-specific limited drops (approved case by case)
